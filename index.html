We believe every dog, cat, and companion animal deserves the same quality of coordinated care that the best human healthcare systems provide.

What we stand for

Our values.

🔬
Science first
Every feature is built on veterinary science. Our educational content is reviewed by licensed DVMs. We never guess about pet health.
🤝
Trust by design
Privacy is not an afterthought. Owners control their data. Access is tiered and transparent. Security is a core feature, not a box to check.
❤️
Pets above profit
We will never monetize pet health data in ways that compromise care. The interests of the pet come first — always.
🌱
Built to last
Chips are permanent. Records should be too. We're building infrastructure for a 20-year relationship, not a feature that disappears in an app update.
🌍
Access for everyone
Core identification features are always free. The basic promise of PetVault — reunite a lost pet — costs nothing to use.
🚀
Relentlessly improving
We ship fast, listen to veterinarians, and iterate continuously. The best version of PetVault is always the next one.
